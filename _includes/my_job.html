My Job

As the Head of Partnerships at the Centre for Environmental Data Analysis (CEDA) I have an exciting and varied role that consists of technical leadership, software development and managing data systems.

Technical leadership and management

I have provided technical leadership on numerous national and international projects. My roles have included person, project and task management.

Software Development

I have 20 years of software development experience. My main expertise lies in working with Python and numerous libraries and frameworks for working with data and developing web applications. I also have experience of databases, Perl, PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and some web frameworks. I have a good knowledge of modern software development principles such as version control, testing and continuous integration.

Data expertise and workflows

Having worked in an environmental data centre for many years, I have a detailed understanding of the issues of data preparation, curation, cataloguing, discovery, access, processing and usage. I also know the importance of making data FAIR and the role of Open Science to drive the agenda for responsible creation and management of data.
